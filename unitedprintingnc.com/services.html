Offset Printing

Whether you need single color or full-color process printing, United Printing provides the options and quality to best fit your requirements and deadlines. For mid-to-long print runs, offset printing – traditional printing done with plates is often the best method to choose. Offset printing allows many creative options to how a project can be printed. From single color, precision multi-color registration, heavy ink coverage or full bleeds United Printing has the solution.

 

Digital Solutions

Using advanced technology we have the ability to print variable data, variable imaging as well as static short run digital on our Igen 4 Digital Press with the Fiery System 10 Platform. There is no compromising on short run work. Greater response rates by targeting specific customer groups. Lower cost short runs…use digital printing for quantities that aren’t cost effective for traditional offset printing. Reduce your print inventory and storage space…print only as much as you need and keep documents on file for future and easy updates.

 

Wide Format Printing

Bring us your dreams, ideas and we will help create innovative solutions customized to your needs. One store or a chain of locations, we create high-impact in-store visuals to increase sales and promote brand. We provide all the resources needed for design, manufacturing, distribution, installation and project management. We create creative window and wall displays, signage, indoor or outdoor signage, on standard substrates or specialty substrates. Think of it and we’ll produce it.

Design

United Printing offers our customers a full-service design and prepress department. Whether you need a logo, brochure, business card, direct mail marketing or a form, our team of Designers and Prepress Technicians can assist with every aspect of your project. From conceptualization to final production, our staff can create the art, scan photos if needed, layout the design and then output the media. Customers have access to our FTP site for file transfer.

 

Mailing Services/Shipping

With postal regulations becoming more complicated, the slightest error in preparation of artwork can add significant charges to your mailing. Let us offer you suggestions on how to design your project to obtain the best mailing rates through automation for First-Class Presort, Standard (Bulk), or Nonprofit mailing permits. Our complete mail services include mail list maintenance, CASS (Coding Accuracy Support System) NCOA certification. Predetermining duplicate address records, and finding undeliverable address records in your data base.  Whether it’s shipping to one location or many locations, we have the resources to get it there.
